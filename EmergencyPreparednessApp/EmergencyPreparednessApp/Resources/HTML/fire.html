Instructions:

  • Remain Calm

  • Shout "Fire, Fire, Fire" to draw attention to the threat

  • Activate the nearest pull station, usually by an exit door

  • Only attempt to fight the fire if you feel confident in your ability to extinguish it, you have the correct fire extinguisher, and a clear exit path.

  • Exit immediately by the nearest unblocked stairwell

  • Call 911 when safe to do so

  • Report to your assembly/muster point and await further direction

  • Do not re-enter the building until given the all clear by authorities

  • When the alarm sounds, exit the building immediately. Some buildings may have a two stage alarm system. Follow local directions form your Floor wardens.

  • Assist those with mobility issues to seek an area of refuge near or in stairwells, assign a "buddy" and alert authorities of the situation. Do not attempt to carry anyone down the stairs unless there is an immediate threat to life.
