The 911 service is there to support you during traumatic events.

Call 911 immediately If you are :

  • Injured or at immediate risk of being injured

  • There is an immediate threat to yourself or others

  • If you are concerned that an event could turn violent

  • Call for help, alert your supervisor, peers or Floor Wardens if available

  • Remove yourself from the threat if possible

  • Once the event is under control contact Security and your department safety office or Health and safety committee.
