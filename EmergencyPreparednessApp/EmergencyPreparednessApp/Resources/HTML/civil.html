If a Demonstration Occurs Outside the Building or in the Lobby

  • Do not, under any circumstances, involve yourself with the demonstrators either verbally or physically.

  • If the demonstrators prevent entry to your work location, withdraw and inform your supervisor.

  • Wait for further direction.

If the Building is Occupied

  • Immediately secure all sensitive documentation and equipment.

  • Do not say or do anything that will agitate the occupiers.

  • Do not attempt to physically restrain the occupiers.

  • Contact your Supervisor and Corporate Security. Wait for further direction.
