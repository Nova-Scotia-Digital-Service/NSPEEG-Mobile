Working Alone

During regular working hours the Floor Emergency Wardens are available to assist you and direct the response to crisis events. Outside of regular hours, at remote locations, and while on the road, employees are reminded to have a "working alone" plan. Your supervisor should be aware of your work location and you should be aware of supports available to you to respond to unusual events.

Contact your Manager

In all events your manager must be notified as soon as possible. If your building has security personnel notify them as well.

You first point of contact for emergency events should be your manager, then building security if available. We suggest having these numbers available on your phone. You can do this by:

  1. Launch the phone application and click on favorites in the lower left corner.

  2. Tap the + button in the top right.

  3. Find the contact you wish to add as a favorite and tap on their name.

  4. Select the information you wish to favorite (phone number, email, FaceTime, etc).
