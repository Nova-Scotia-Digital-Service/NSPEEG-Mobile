Power failure

  1. Set all light fixtures, equipment, and appliances switches to the OFF position to protect them when the power comes back on.

  2. During normal hours remain in place and await instructions from your Manager, Floor Emergency Wardens or Security.

  3. Outside of regular hours you are encouraged to evacuate the building immediately. Emergency lighting will last only up to30min.

  4. Do not attempt to use the elevator. Report all persons who may be trapped in elevators.

  5. Alert your supervisor.

Elevator malfunction

  1. Use emergency call button or phone inside the elevator, or a cell phone, to contact building maintenance and/or security.

  2. Give your name and contact information, and specific location ‑ building, elevator location or number, floor.

  3. Wait for rescue. Be patient. Technician has to get to you, then take time to get you out.

  4. Reassure others trapped with you, ask them to stay calm, and tell them what you have learned about the rescue plan.
