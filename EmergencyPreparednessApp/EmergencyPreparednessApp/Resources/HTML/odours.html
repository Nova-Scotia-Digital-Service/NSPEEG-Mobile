Unusual Odours

  1. Remain calm - Notify your supervisor and Building Maintenance of the situation, and allow them to investigate further.

  2. Outside of regular hours you are advised to contact your supervisor. If you feel there is an immediate threat within the building, evacuate and call 911.

  3. Instructions during normal hours will vary depending on situation, they may include evacuation, shelter in place, or no action. You should be ready to respond.

Chemical Release

  1. Sometimes odours may be a sign of a significant hazard. .I.e. natural gas

  2. Remain calm.

  3. Stay where you are and enact shelter in place procedures.

  4. Remain in your building and monitor emails and phone calls for further instructions.
